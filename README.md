## Otherwise known as

Those new to the EA or Defra may well be surprised by the explosion üí• of acronyms used! There are a number you may come across that are all used to refer to this service.

- **WABS** Water Abstraction Service
- **WAL** Water Abstraction Licence (commonly used by our web-ops team)
- **WRLS** Water Resource Licence Service
